What We Offer

Comprehensive dental care
for every patient

From your child's first dental visit to advanced restorative procedures, our experienced team provides personalised, gentle care across all areas of dentistry.

General Dentistry

Routine check-ups, professional cleaning (scaling & polishing), emergency care, and extractions for adults and children.

Paediatric Dentistry

Kid-friendly dental care with a focus on behaviour management, preventive procedures, and gentle emergency treatments.

Restorative Dentistry

Tooth-coloured fillings, root canal therapy, crowns, bridges, and amalgam restorations to restore full function and appearance.

Orthodontics

Traditional braces and personalised teeth-straightening solutions for a perfectly aligned smile at any age.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Laser teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, dental masking, tooth gems, and premium cosmetic crowns.

Prosthetic Dentistry

Full and partial dentures to replace missing teeth, restore chewing function, and improve overall oral health.

Periodontology

Comprehensive gum care including diagnosis, treatment planning, and maintenance procedures like scaling and polishing.

Oral Surgery

Minor oral surgery, difficult extractions, management of jaw fractures, and diagnosis of complex oral conditions.

Community Dentistry

Routine fluoride treatments, patient education, and preventive outreach programmes promoting proper at-home oral care.

About Us

Caring for smiles across Kenya


Pine Dental Consultants is a trusted dental clinic with branches in Thika and Kitengela, dedicated to providing high-quality dental care for patients of all ages — from the very young to seniors.

Our team of qualified dental professionals combines clinical expertise with a warm, welcoming approach to ensure every visit is as comfortable as possible. We believe healthy teeth are the foundation of overall wellbeing.
